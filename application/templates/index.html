About Us

AcademicAce is an online hub for students and tutors to exchange materials and information related to lectures of a particular course. This platform provides a convenient and organized way for both parties to stay up-to-date and be engaged with the course material. It simplifies the process of sharing and accessing relevant information, fostering collaboration and facilitating communication between students and tutors.
